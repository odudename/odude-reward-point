Connect your WordPress site and WooCommerce store to the ODude Reward Point Ledger to award and redeem points.

== Description ==

Want to keep your website visitors coming back and boost your online sales? 

**ODude Reward Point** allows you to add a powerful, secure loyalty program to your WordPress site and WooCommerce store. By connecting your site to the ODude Reward Point Ledger, you can reward your users with points for being active and let customers use those points for discounts when they shop!

Here is why you and your visitors will love it:

### 🌟 Reward Users for Being Active
Encourage people to engage with your website! You can automatically reward users with points when they:
* **Create an account** (Welcome gift!)
* **Log in daily** (Keep them coming back every day)
* **Write comments** (Boost discussion on your blog posts)

### 🛍️ Supercharge Your WooCommerce Sales
Turn one-time shoppers into loyal customers:
* **Earn on purchase:** Customers automatically get points when they buy products from your store.
* **Easy discounts at checkout:** Shoppers can redeem their points for a discount on their cart with a single click.
* **Flexible controls:** Set custom earning rates, limit the maximum discount per order, or exclude tax and shipping.

### 🌐 A Truly Universal Reward Network
This plugin is more than just a points ledger—it connects you to a truly universal loyalty network:
* **For Store Owners:** Manage advanced rules, campaigns, and stats through the remote platform at [point.odude.com](https://point.odude.com/).
* **For Customers:** Shoppers can access their own dedicated portal at `name.odude.com` to check balances and transaction histories across different sites.
* **Mobile App Integration:** Customers can download the mobile app to view their points in real-time and get instant push notifications whenever they earn points at your store.

### ⚡ Lightning Fast & Secure
* **No website slowdowns:** All the heavy calculations are handled securely off-site, and points are cached locally so your website stays super fast.
* **Beautiful dashboard:** See your connection status and quick stats directly inside your WordPress admin dashboard.
* **Simple shortcodes:** Easily show users their current points and history anywhere on your site using simple copy-paste codes.

== Installation ==

Ready to get started? It takes less than 2 minutes:

1. **Upload** the plugin files to your `/wp-content/plugins/` folder, or install it directly through your WordPress admin panel.
2. **Activate** the plugin.
3. Click on the new **ODude Reward Point** menu in your WordPress sidebar.
4. Enter your ODude API Endpoint and Merchant **Secret Key** to connect.
5. Set how many points users should get, and you're good to go!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need WooCommerce to use this plugin? =
No! WooCommerce is completely optional. If you don't have a shop, you can still use the plugin to reward users for registering, logging in, and posting comments.

= How do my visitors see their points? =
You can display their points balance and history anywhere on your site using these simple codes:
* `[odude_reward_point_balance]` - Shows their current point balance.
* `[odude_reward_point_history]` - Shows a clean list of all the points they have earned and spent.

= Is it fast? =
Yes! The plugin stores points details locally on your site using smart caching so it doesn't have to talk to the server every time a page loads. Your site stays fast.
